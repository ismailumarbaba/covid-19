About DSC FULafia

Developer Student Clubs, Federal University of Lafia (DSC FULafia) are university based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome.

By joining a DSC FULafia, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
